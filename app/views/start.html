Use this service to register buildings that satisfy at least one of the criteria below:

  • the building is over 18m high and has at least 2 residential units
  • the building has 6 or more storeys and has at least 2 residential units

If you have all the information to hand, this registration service will take around 2 hours.

The information required

The Building Safety Act requires the accountable person for a High Rise Building to gather certain types of information about the building. You'll need some of this information to register your building.

If the building you're responsible for is older, or has changed ownership multiple times, some of the information you'll need may not be readily available. You’ll need to take reasonable steps to find the required information.

When gathering information about your building, you may find problems. This gives you the chance to do something about them before an incident occurs.

Before you start

Once registered, your building will display in your Building Safety Regulator portal. Accountable persons are required to update the information submitted where it changes.

You'll need to pay a £55 online fee to complete this registration service.

You can also register by post.
